Canarino Cioccolato is a coffee & chocolate company whose identity is inspired by Italian design of the late 60’s and 70’s. The patterns and palettes are derived from the traditional espresso houses of Rome and their use of African patterns and imagery. The canary mark pays homage to the bold poster art that dominated advertising design during the mid century period. 

The Canarino Coffee House offers a range of African, Javanese and Sumatran blends as well as its own signature cold brew iced coffee. Each blend is designated a specific number.
